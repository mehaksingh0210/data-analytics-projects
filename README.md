# Customer Behavior Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes customer shopping behavior using a retail shopping dataset from Kaggle. The objective is to identify purchasing patterns, customer segments, shopping preferences, and actionable business insights through Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA).

This project was completed as part of the Data Analytics Internship Program.

---

## Objectives

* Analyze customer purchasing behavior.
* Identify popular product categories.
* Study customer demographics and shopping preferences.
* Evaluate payment method usage.
* Analyze revenue trends across shopping malls and time periods.
* Segment customers based on spending behavior.
* Generate business recommendations from data insights.

---

## Dataset

Source: Kaggle Customer Shopping Dataset

Dataset Features:

* Invoice Number
* Customer ID
* Gender
* Age
* Product Category
* Quantity Purchased
* Price
* Payment Method
* Invoice Date
* Shopping Mall

Total Records: 99,457

## Project Workflow

### 1. Data Collection

Downloaded the customer shopping dataset from Kaggle.

### 2. Data Cleaning

* Checked for missing values
* Checked for duplicate records
* Converted date columns to datetime format

### 3. Feature Engineering

* Created Total Sales feature
* Extracted month information from transaction dates

### 4. Exploratory Data Analysis

* Gender Distribution
* Age Distribution
* Product Category Analysis
* Revenue by Category
* Payment Method Analysis
* Shopping Mall Performance
* Monthly Sales Trends
* Correlation Analysis

### 5. Customer Segmentation

Customers were grouped into:

* Low Value Customers
* Medium Value Customers
* High Value Customers

### 6. Business Recommendations

Generated actionable insights based on customer purchasing patterns and revenue trends.

---

## Key Insights

* Certain product categories contribute significantly more revenue than others.
* Shopping malls show noticeable differences in sales performance.
* Customer spending behavior varies across age groups.
* Digital payment methods are widely adopted.
* A small group of customers contributes a large share of total revenue.

---

## Business Recommendations

* Focus marketing efforts on top-performing product categories.
* Introduce loyalty programs for high-value customers.
* Run promotional campaigns during lower sales periods.
* Encourage digital payment adoption through incentives.
* Use customer segmentation for personalized marketing.
